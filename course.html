LIBRARY

The Jaya College of Arts and Science library is well established as a knowledge source for the students of the different disciplines and faculty to obtain information. It is spacious and the resources are made easily accessible by the serene ambiance and effective computerization. Our library is housed in a hall of 8100sqft with a variegated collection of over 10121 titles 21678 Volumes apart from subscriptions to leading national and international periodicals and journals. It has a seating capacity of 150 students at a time. Leading newspapers, Magazines, and Journals are subscribed regularly to augment the library as a rich source of knowledge. The library is equipped with 10 computers for access to web resources. It also includes general knowledge books, books related to competitive examinations, and spiritual and religious books. The Digital library available for the students with individual login ID and password is useful to access the internet materials
