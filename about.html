ABOUT US

JAYA COLLEGE OF ARTS AND SCIENCES

Jaya College of Arts & Science is an institute of higher education that has received approval from the University of Madras and thus operates within the structure of the Tamil Nadu Educational Code for self- supporting private colleges. Enhancing knowledge, improving skills and increasing participation in the academic programmers are the noble objectives of this college.
Jaya College of Arts & Science, Thiruninravur, Tamil Nadu, comprises several divisions, each offering different courses for students. Division of Life Sciences, Division of Computing, Division of Hotel & Catering Management, Division of Business Management and Commerce, Division of Pure Sciences, and Division of Applied Sciences are the names of various divisions in the curriculum. Students can pursue both undergraduate and postgraduate courses that are offered by this college.
Jaya College of Arts & Science is quite beneficial for the students. It has a huge collection of books, including 1000 volumes, good periodicals, wide-ranging reference books and computer-based information services. One also can get help from its collection of textbooks, college catalogues and previous examination papers. In addition the library subscribes to multitudes of professional journals of international fame. The library also offers certain on-line services like accessing searchable databases, electronic journals. Browsing several useful sites via internet too can be accomplished.
Every department has its own laboratory that is well-equipped. The central laboratory of Information Technology facilitates teaching on various subjects. In this manner all these modern laboratories provide an outstanding facility for learning engineering skills that are required for engineers of present day. The college provides dormitory housing for students at affordable rates.
Although stress is put on improving the standards of education, yet it is not that the other aspects of campus life are neglected. In fact Jaya College Of Arts & Science tries to inculcate the spirit of involvement with the community at large, serving people and enhancing their educational experience beyond campus, thereby increasing their social consciousness on charitable basis. Career Development Services of Jaya College of Arts & Science shoulders the responsibility of fostering career opportunities, assisting them to search jobs etc
The College located in the lap of natural beauty and glory, is situated in C.T.H. Road, Thiruninravur (Near Avadi), Chennai. For smooth transportation, it has an excellent road link between the Thiruninravur town and Chennai and ahead. Special fares and discounts are available on all transports that the college arranges for its students to reach destinations in Chennai.
