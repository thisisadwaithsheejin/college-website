LIBRARY

The Jaya College of Arts and Science library is well established as a knowledge source for the students of the different disciplines and faculty to obtain information. It is spacious and the resources are made easily accessible by the serene ambiance and effective computerization. Our library is housed in a hall of 8100sqft with a variegated collection of over 10121 titles 21678 Volumes apart from subscriptions to leading national and international periodicals and journals. It has a seating capacity of 150 students at a time. Leading newspapers, Magazines, and Journals are subscribed regularly to augment the library as a rich source of knowledge. The library is equipped with 10 computers for access to web resources. It also includes general knowledge books, books related to competitive examinations, and spiritual and religious books. The Digital library available for the students with individual login ID and password is useful to access the internet materials

NCC/NSS/YRC/RRC

The Jaya NCC has presently has one NCC unit namely 13 TN BN NCC (SD & SW) at Madras B Group, Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry and Andaman Nicobar Directorate. The Present Jaya NCC if functioning with the strength of 01 ANO and 52 Cadets under13 TN BN NCC. The NCC units of Jaya provide ample opportunities to develop one’s physical and intellectual potential through different training camps and activities like Republic Day Camp (RDC), Thalsanik Camp (TSC) Advanced Leadership Camp (ALC), Basic Leadership Camp (BLC), Compined Annual Training Camp(CATC), Army Attachment Training Camp (AATC), Youth Exchange Programme (YEP) , Rock Climbing Training Camp(RCTC), Special National Integration Camp ( SNIC), community development program cum social service activities like sapling plantations, blood donation camps, social awareness rallies, cleanliness drives of statues, COVID-199 related camps, adventure training like trekking camp sports camp etc. along with institutional trainings including Foot Drill, Arms Drill, Weapons training, Obstacles Training, Self Defense, Map Reading Etc.
